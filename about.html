Learn About Us

The organization was established in June 2020. It operate within Nyanfeku Ekroful in the Abura-Asebu-Kwamankese District which is found in the central part of Ghana. It operations expands beyond it surrounding and accross regions of Ghana and outdide Ghana. Molex Foundation Africa seeks to provide coaching and mentorship to the upcoming ones in the society. It also to seek to project how important a girl child is in nation development in others words it seek to empower the girl child. Molex Foundation Africa also help the upcoming ones in the society by providing or exposing them to career guidance that will enable to pursue their dreams whiles growing up etc. The management team consist of Dr. Patrick Essien who is the CEO and a lecturer at the University of Capen Coast (FOUNDER), Mr Ato-Turkson Daniel a student at the University of Ghana reading B.A Accounting and Sociology (Co-Founder and Directoer of Operations), Mr Dominic Ebow Arhin who is a student of University of Ghana reading Political Science and Information Studies (Co-Founder and Communication Director), Mr Emmanuel Kuta Essien a graduate of the University of Education Winneba who studied Business Administration in Accounting (Director of finance), Charlotte Owusu Ansah (Director in charge of Gender), Prince Nana Tsiwah a student University of Cape Coast reading Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (IT Director) and Ms Lovelace Amos a graduate from the University of Cape Coast (Project and Innovation Director).
